## Business Understanding

### Model de Negocio

**Problem solved:** Tooth loss and misalignment affect hundreds of millions globally, with only 16 million of ~220 million potential patients receiving implant treatment annually. Straumann provides the complete dental restoration solution: implants, prosthetics, digital workflows, and clear aligners.

**How it makes money:**

| Segment | Revenue % | Description | Margins |
|---------|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Implantology | ~55% | Dental implants + prosthetic components | Highest (premium brand) |
| Prosthetics | ~15% | Crowns, abutments, restorative materials | High (recurring) |
| Orthodontics | ~15% | ClearCorrect aligners | Lower (scale-up phase) |
| Digital | ~15% | Scanners (SIRIOS), software (AXS platform) | Improving |

**Revenue model:** Largely consumable/device -- each implant procedure requires the implant + prosthetic components. Repeat patient need (multi-tooth) and growing patient pool create organic growth. Digital workflows (AXS cloud platform) add recurring software revenue.

**Recurring revenue characteristics:**
- Prosthetic components are consumed per procedure (recurring)
- Dentists trained on Straumann systems have high switching costs (surgeon training model similar to ISRG)
- 10,700 education programs training 370,000 professionals in 2025 deepens lock-in
- AXS cloud platform creates digital workflow dependency

### Unit Economics

- **Gross Margin: 71.2%** (FY2024), declining from 76.2% (2021)
  - Mix shift toward ClearCorrect (lower GM) and value segment (Neodent in EM)
  - Core implant margins remain 75%+
