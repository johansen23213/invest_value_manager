## META-REFLECTION

### Dudas sobre esta decision
- **The FV of CHF 110 converges with analyst consensus (CHF 108).** This means our informational edge is slim. The thesis is "buy quality at reasonable price" — a sound approach but not one where we have a strong differentiated view. If the consensus is wrong (e.g., GM compression accelerates, China VBP worse than expected), both we and the consensus will be wrong.
- **The fill probability of 25-35% means this SO may never execute.** CHF 80 is 3.6% below the 52-week low. It requires a new low, which only happens with material bad news (tariff escalation, H1 miss, sector-wide sell-off). We should be comfortable with the possibility that this opportunity passes us by.
- **FCF margin assumption of 13% is still an INFERENCE**, not a management-guided figure. If the company enters a new capex cycle (acquisitions, new factory), FCF could stay at 11% indefinitely.

### Debilidades del analisis recibido
- **No separate moat_assessment.md or risk_assessment.md.** This was a fast-track R1, which means the adversarial coverage relies entirely on R2. A full pipeline with three separate agents (moat, risk, fundamental) would have produced deeper coverage.
- **China VBP data is limited.** The R2 cited Dental Tribune and Mirae Asset research, but the R1 relied primarily on company disclosures. Independent verification of VBP 2.0 impact was not fully achieved.
- **yfinance data anomalies.** Dividend yield showing 98-100% is a clear data error. FCF CAGR of -10.4% is misleading (capex cycle, not operational deterioration). These anomalies in the tools require awareness and manual correction.

### Sugerencias de mejora
- **DCF tool WACC override.** Both R1 and R2 noted the tool's beta-driven WACC (12.6%) produces unreliable results for Swiss-listed medtech. Consider adding a --wacc flag to dcf_calculator.py.
- **Mandatory FCF normalization disclosure.** When an analyst projects FCF margin improvement > 200bps vs trailing, the thesis should document the specific pathway and whether management has guided to it.
- **GM AMBER monitoring protocol.** For positions with kill conditions within 200bps of trigger, consider a formal monitoring checklist at each earnings date.

### Preguntas para Orchestrator
- None. The analysis is complete and the standing order is ready for activation with the GM >= 70% condition. The SO should be added to standing_orders.yaml with GATED status.
