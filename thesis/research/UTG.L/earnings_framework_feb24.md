## 3. ESCENARIOS DE EARNINGS

### BULL (probabilidad 20%)
**Occupancy FY2025 >96%, FY2026/27 reservations >70%, synergies ahead**

Que buscar:
- FY2025 occupancy comes in at 96%+ (above H1 95.2%)
- FY2027 reservations >=70% (vs 64% earlier)
- Empiric synergies >GBP 13.7M guided
- Share buyback progress update (GBP 100M program)
- Immigration policy commentary less negative than feared
- LTV stable at 26% or declining

**Implicacion:** Trough shallower than feared. FV thesis validated. Consider LOWERING entry trigger from 540p to 560-570p.

### BASE (probabilidad 50%)
**Occupancy 95-96%, reservations ~65%, guidance maintained**

Que buscar:
- FY2025 occupancy 95-96% (in-line with H1 trajectory)
- FY2026 guidance: EPS -7 to -10% (as guided)
- FY2026 occupancy target 93-96% maintained
- Empiric integration on-track, synergies 13.7M confirmed
- Dividend flat at ~37.3p
- EPRA NTA broadly stable

**Implicacion:** Thesis intacta. FY2026 is trough year. Standing order at 540p MAINTAINED.

### BEAR (probabilidad 30%)
**Occupancy <94%, reservations declining, guidance cut**

Que buscar:
- FY2025 occupancy <94.5%
- FY2027 reservations <60%
- FY2026 EPS decline >-10%
- Empiric integration delays
- LTV rising above 28%
- Immigration commentary signaling further restrictions
- Dividend at risk

**Implicacion:** Thesis debilitada. LOWER standing order to 500-510p. If KC triggered, REMOVE from pipeline.
