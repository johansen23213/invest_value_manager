- DOGE contract terminations have continued, reinforcing the structural rather than cyclical interpretation

When your contrarian thesis becomes consensus, the edge disappears. We need Roper to DEMONSTRATE per-seat revenue resilience, not just argue it theoretically.

4. **The DCF tool's base case of $291 contradicts our $365 FV.** This gap ($74, or 25%) reflects different assumptions about growth and WACC. Our derived parameters are more Roper-specific but the tool provides a useful reality check.
