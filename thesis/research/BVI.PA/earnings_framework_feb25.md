## 3. ESCENARIOS DE EARNINGS

### BULL (probabilidad 20%)
**Organic growth >7%, margin expansion >50bps, strong FY2026 guidance**

Que buscar:
- FY2025 organic growth >7% (above guidance)
- Adj. op margin >16.5%
- FCF >EUR 920M (new record)
- LEAP|28 targets reaffirmed or raised
- FY2026 guidance: organic growth 7-9%
- Marine & Offshore continues double-digit growth
- Certification/cybersecurity divisions accelerating
- Wendel update: further stake reduction without price disruption

**Implicacion:** R3 FV of EUR 29 becomes conservative. Consider revising upward toward EUR 33 (pre-adversarial). Entry rises to EUR 24. FAST-TRACK R4 committee.

### BASE (probabilidad 50%)
**Organic growth 6-7%, modest margin expansion, guidance maintained**

Que buscar:
- FY2025 organic growth 6-7% (in-line with 9M trajectory)
- Adj. op margin 16.0-16.3%
- FCF EUR 860-920M
- LEAP|28 targets confirmed
- FY2026 guidance: mid-to-high single-digit organic growth
- Margin expansion trajectory intact
- Wendel stake update (any block sales completed/planned)

**Implicacion:** Thesis validated. Standing order EUR 22 MAINTAINED. R4 committee can proceed post-earnings but NOT URGENT (price well above entry).

### BEAR (probabilidad 25%)
**Organic growth <5%, margin stagnation, or negative guidance**

Que buscar:
- FY2025 organic growth <5.5%
- Adj. op margin declining or flat at 16.0%
- FCF <EUR 850M
- Marine & Offshore growth decelerating sharply
- Consumer Products weakness deepening
- FY2026 guidance: low single-digit growth
- Governance issues: China corruption investigation update
- Wendel accelerating exit (fire sale)

**Implicacion:** R3 FV EUR 29 remains appropriate. LOWER entry to EUR 20. Deprioritize further vs Tier A candidates. Monitor but don't advance.

### STRUCTURAL BEAR (probabilidad 5%)
**Kill conditions approaching**

Que buscar:
- Organic growth negative
- Operating margin contracts >200bps
- Major regulatory scandal
- ROIC approaches WACC
- Dividend cut

**Implicacion:** ARCHIVE. Remove from pipeline.
