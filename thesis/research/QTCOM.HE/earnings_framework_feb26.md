## 3. ESCENARIOS DE EARNINGS

### BULL (probabilidad 15%)
**Q4 organic revenue > EUR 55M, EBITA margin recovering, FY2026 guidance 10%+ organic growth**

Que buscar:
- Q4 organic revenue (ex-IAR) >EUR 55M (indicating recovery started)
- Q4 EBITA margin >15% (sequential improvement from 10.5% Q3)
- FY2026 organic growth guidance >=10%
- FY2026 EBITA margin guidance >=25%
- IAR SaaS transition plan with clear metrics
- No third profit warning

**Implicacion:** Recovery confirmed early. Advance to R4 committee with FV EUR 32-35. Entry EUR 24-26.

### BASE (probabilidad 40%)
**In-line with guided expectations. FY2026 shows modest improvement but uncertainty remains.**

Que buscar:
- Q4 organic revenue EUR 50-55M
- FY2025 EBITA margin 16-20% full year
- FY2026 guidance: organic growth 5-8%
- FY2026 EBITA margin guidance 20-25%
- IAR integration progressing, some SaaS visibility
- Revenue growth resuming but below peak levels

**Implicacion:** CONTINUE WATCHLIST. Not enough for R4. Monitor Q1 2026 for confirmation. Entry at EUR 20-22.

### BEAR (probabilidad 35%)
**Q4 deteriorates further, third profit warning, or guidance disappointing**

Que buscar:
- Q4 organic revenue <EUR 48M
- FY2025 EBITA margin <16%
- Third profit warning for FY2026
- FY2026 organic growth guidance <5%
- FY2026 EBITA margin guidance <18%
- IAR integration problems
- Revenue decline in ANY segment

**Implicacion:** R3 bear scenario materializes. FV drops to EUR 18-22. ARCHIVE or lower entry dramatically.

### STRUCTURAL IMPAIRMENT (probabilidad 10%)
**Fundamental business deterioration**

Que buscar:
- Revenue decline (not just growth deceleration)
- EBITA margin <15% with no recovery path
- Customer losses to competitors (especially Unreal Engine)
- IAR write-down or impairment
- Qt for MCU/Qt for Device Creation losing share

**Implicacion:** ARCHIVE immediately. Remove from pipeline.

## 4. HARD GATE CRITERIA (from R3 resolution)

### PASS — Advance to R4:
1. Q4 2025 organic revenue recovery (>EUR 50M, ex-IAR)
2. FY2026 organic growth guidance 10%+ (strong recovery) or 5%+ (minimum)
3. EBITA margin guidance 25%+ for FY2026
4. No third profit warning

### FAIL — Archive or lower entry:
- FY2026 organic growth <5% → REMOVE from pipeline
- EBITA margin <15% sustained → ARCHIVE
- Third profit warning → ARCHIVE
- Revenue decline → ARCHIVE

### ADDITIONAL GATES (from R3):
- eToro tradability — VERIFY BEFORE R4
- QS re-scored with FY2025 annual data
- IAR SaaS transformation plan visibility
