## 3. SCENARIOS

### BULL (probability 25%)
**PBT > GBP 79M, strong FY2026 guidance, laundry >1,200 installations**

What to look for:
- PBT >= GBP 80M (beat guidance top end)
- Laundry installations >= 1,200 (or acceleration in pace)
- FY2026 revenue guidance >GBP 335M (+6%+)
- German next-gen photobooth certification confirmed
- Succession plan articulated (Vladimir Crasnianski as Deputy CEO doing well)
- Receivables growth explained satisfactorily
- Special dividend or enhanced buyback

**Implication:** Thesis STRONGLY confirmed. Create standing order at R3 entry price. Priority pipeline candidate.

### BASE (probability 55%)
**PBT GBP 76-79M in-line, laundry 1,000-1,200 installations, standard guidance**

What to look for:
- PBT within guidance range
- Laundry installs 1,000-1,200 (on track)
- FY2026 guidance GBP 325-340M (+3-8%)
- Photobooth revenue stable (not declining >2%)
- Dividend maintained or modest increase
- No succession surprises
- German market commentary shows awareness of regulation impact

**Implication:** Thesis intact. Proceed with R3/R4 pipeline. Create standing order.

### BEAR (probability 20%)
**PBT < GBP 76M, laundry stall, or photobooth decline**

What to look for:
- PBT below guidance (<GBP 76M)
- Laundry installations < 800 (rollout stalling)
- Photobooth revenue declining >5%
- FY2026 guidance flat or down
- German next-gen kiosks NOT certified (digital regulation = headwind not tailwind)
- Receivables issue = collection problem not timing
- CEO health/succession issue
- Dividend cut

**Implication:** Re-evaluate thesis. Potential PAUSE or CANCEL pipeline.
