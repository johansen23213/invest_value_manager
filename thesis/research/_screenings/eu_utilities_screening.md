## Risk Analysis - Sector Level

### Macro Risks
1. **Interest Rates**: Fed/ECB en pausa (3.5-3.75% US, 2.0% EU), pero no bajarán pronto → utilities sin viento a favor de re-rating. High debt sensitivity.
2. **Recession Risk**: US/EU crecimiento débil 2% / 1.2% → demand risk electricidad, aunque utilities defensivas.
3. **Regulatory**: Windfall taxes (Spain, Italy ya visto), price caps, Ofgem reviews (UK) = cash flow volatility.

### Sector-Specific Risks
1. **Capex Intensity**: Renewables transition requiere capex masivo (típicamente OCF coverage 100-120%) → FCF bajo, dividendos en riesgo si ejecución falla.
2. **Commodity Exposure**: Gas utilities (Naturgy, Engie parcial) expuestas a gas demand decline estructural (electrificación, eficiencia).
3. **Technology Disruption**: Distributed generation (solar rooftop), batteries erosionan monopolio utilities tradicionales.
4. **Geopolitical**:
   - Gas supply (Russia/Ukraine, Norway dependency)
   - China competition (renewable equipment, steel)
   - Political populism (LATAM for Enel/Iberdrola, UK Labour for National Grid)

### Company-Specific Red Flags
- **Naturgy**: Yield 9.3% si cierto = payout >100%? Value trap risk.
- **Enel**: Brasil concessions (Ceará halted, São Paulo pending) = 20-30% revenue risk.
- **EDP**: P/E 24x trailing = requiere earnings perfection.
- **Orsted**: Offshore wind distress = avoid.
