## Business Understanding

### What Belimo Does

Belimo is the global #1 manufacturer of **field devices for HVAC systems**: actuators (the "muscles" that open/close dampers and valves), control valves (regulate water/air flow), and sensors/meters. These are mission-critical components in every commercial building's heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system.

**Revenue split (FY2024):**
- Damper Actuators: ~42% of sales
- Control Valves: ~53% of sales (fastest growing)
- Sensors & Meters: ~5% of sales

**Geographic split (H1 2025):**
- Americas: 50% (growing 30%+ driven by data center boom)
- EMEA: 39% (growing ~10%)
- Asia Pacific: 12% (growing ~21%)

### Why This Business is Exceptional

1. **Niche dominance:** 21% global market share in HVAC actuators (#1 globally, vs Honeywell #2 at 17%). Unlike Siemens/Honeywell/JCI that sell entire building management systems, Belimo is a pure-play specialist in field devices.

2. **Razor-razorblade economics:** Actuators and valves are installed in building HVAC systems that last 20+ years. Replacement and retrofit creates recurring demand. The products are small-ticket (typically <1% of total HVAC project cost) but mission-critical -- "must-have" not "nice-to-have."

3. **Switching costs:** Once engineers spec Belimo devices into a building design (via CAD/BIM tools), the entire control system is designed around them. Changing mid-project is extremely costly. Belimo's award-winning digital tools (BIM data) reinforce this lock-in.

4. **Pricing power:** Gross margin 61.6% and expanding. In a sector where median is 28%. This is +33.6pp premium -- the highest GM premium of any company I've analyzed. Products are <1% of project cost but critical, so price sensitivity is minimal.

5. **Data center inflection:** Data center cooling has gone from ~10% of sales (H1 2024) to ~18% (H2 2025). Belimo's electronic pressure-independent valves (EPIVs) and energy valves are becoming standard in liquid cooling for AI server racks. This is a structural shift, not cyclical.

### Unit Economics

- FCF margin: 14% (FY2024), improving from 6.9% in 2022 (supply chain normalization)
- Capex intensity: Currently elevated at 6.2% of sales (capacity expansion for data center demand), expected to normalize to historical levels by 2027
- Asset-light relative to manufacturing peers: intellectual property + brand + channel = moat
- LTV/CAC: Not directly measurable but implied by 60%+ gross margins and 28.9% ROIC

### FY2025 Results (Recently Announced -- Jan 19, 2026)

The data shows FY2025 was exceptional:
- **Revenue: CHF 1,121M** (+23.3% in local currencies, +18.7% in CHF)
- **H1 2025 EBIT margin: 22.8%** (company guided full-year "above 20%")
- **H1 2025 Net Income: CHF 101.3M** (+31.2% YoY)
- **Americas +30%+**, driven by data center demand
- **Control valves +31%** in local currency (data center cooling)

If EBIT margin held above 20% for FY2025, implied EBIT ~ CHF 225-240M and Net Income ~ CHF 180-190M.

Using CHF 180M net income estimate / 12.3M shares = EPS ~CHF 14.6.
At CHF 903: P/E ~ 62x on FY2025 (vs 65x on FY2024 EPS of CHF 11.94).

### Why Is It Expensive?

This is NOT a "why is it cheap" section. Belimo is **extremely expensive**:

1. **Data center AI narrative:** The market is pricing Belimo as an AI/data center infrastructure play. Data center cooling is a real growth driver, but the valuation implies SUSTAINING 47% FCF growth for 5 years.

2. **Momentum:** Stock doubled from CHF 443 to CHF 903 in 12 months. Momentum investors are piling in.

3. **Swiss premium:** Swiss stocks often trade at premium multiples due to quality bias, currency stability, and limited float.

4. **Scarcity value:** There is no comparable pure-play HVAC field device company. Belimo is unique.

5. **Earnings inflection:** FY2025 showed 23% revenue growth and margin expansion -- the best year in company history. Market is extrapolating this forward.

### My Contra-Thesis (Why the Market Could Be Wrong)

The market is NOT wrong about the quality -- Belimo is genuinely exceptional. The market IS wrong about the price:

| Market believes | I believe | Evidence |

| Data center demand sustains 20%+ growth for 5+ years | Data center is real but cyclical; hyperscaler capex follows boom-bust patterns | Historical AI capex cycles (crypto mining, cloud 2015-2018) showed 2-3 year bursts |

| Scarcity premium justified at any price | Even the best business has a price that destroys returns | 47% implied FCF growth is 6x historical rate |

## Preliminary Valuation

### Method 1: Owner Earnings Yield (Primary for Tier A)

```
FY2024 Data:
  FCF: CHF 132M
  Depreciation: ~CHF 28M (implied from capex/dep ratio 2.1x and capex CHF 58.7M)
  Maintenance Capex: ~CHF 31M (Depreciation x 1.1)
  Owner Earnings: CHF 132M - CHF 31M + CHF 28M = CHF 129M

Wait -- FCF already deducts all capex. So:
  Owner Earnings = FCF + Growth Capex
  Total Capex FY2024: CHF 58.7M
  Maintenance Capex (est. Dep x 1.1): ~CHF 31M
  Growth Capex: ~CHF 28M
  Owner Earnings = CHF 132M + CHF 28M = CHF 160M

Market Cap: CHF 11.1B
Owner Earnings Yield = CHF 160M / CHF 11.1B = 1.44%

FY2025 Estimated:
  Revenue: CHF 1,121M
  EBIT margin: ~21% (H1 was 22.8%, full year guided above 20%)
  EBIT: ~CHF 235M
  Tax rate: ~17%
  Net Income: ~CHF 195M
  Capex: ~CHF 60M (elevated)
  D&A: ~CHF 30M
  FCF: ~CHF 165-175M (assumes working capital neutral)
  Maintenance Capex: ~CHF 33M
  Growth Capex: ~CHF 27M
  Owner Earnings: ~CHF 192-202M

  Owner Earnings Yield (FY2025e): ~CHF 197M / CHF 11.1B = 1.77%

Expected Growth: 8-10% long-term (data center adds 3-5pp to historical 5-6%)
Dividend Yield: 1.1%

OEY + Growth + Yield = 1.77% + 9% + 1.1% = 11.9%
WACC: 10.3%

Spread: +1.6pp -- MARGINALLY above WACC.
```

**Interpretation:** At CHF 903, Belimo's total expected return (OEY + growth + yield) barely exceeds its cost of capital. For a Tier A compounder, we need a meaningful spread. This suggests the stock is priced to deliver WACC-like returns, not alpha.

## META-REFLECTION

### Confidence Level
**HIGH confidence in FANTASY verdict.** The quality is undeniable (QS 80, Tier A). The overvaluation is extreme and unambiguous -- no reasonable set of assumptions justifies CHF 903. Even the most aggressive bull case leaves 25%+ downside.

### Incertidumbres/Dudas
- **FY2025 full-year EBIT/net income not yet published** (annual report expected March 2026). I estimated from H1 data and guidance. Actual results could surprise either way, but at 62x earnings, even a 20% earnings beat does not close the valuation gap.
- **Data center cooling TAM is genuinely hard to size.** If Belimo's addressable market in data center cooling is larger than I estimated (e.g., liquid cooling for ALL hyperscaler buildouts globally), the growth runway could be longer. But the stock would need to sustain 40%+ growth for 5+ years to justify current price -- unprecedented for any industrial company.
- **Swiss market dynamics.** BEAN.SW has limited float (20% insider, 48% institutional). Thin float + momentum can sustain high valuations longer than fundamentals warrant. This is not an investability concern but a timing concern -- FANTASY stocks can stay FANTASY for years.

Belimo belongs in a "building-automation" or "industrial-technology" sector view alongside companies like Halma, Spirax, Atlas Copco, Roper Technologies. If we analyze more HVAC/building automation companies, we should create a dedicated sector view.
