## Business Understanding

### Three Segments
1. **EFT Processing** ($1.28B, 30%): Largest independent ATM network in Europe (~57K ATMs). ATM outsourcing, DCC, merchant acquiring. Growing at +11%.
2. **epay** ($1.19B, 28%): Prepaid mobile top-up, digital content distribution. ~363K retailer locations, 59 countries. Low growth +3%.
3. **Money Transfer** ($1.78B, 42%): Ria (#2 global after WU), xe.com, Dandelion platform. 639K locations, 200 countries, 183M transactions.
