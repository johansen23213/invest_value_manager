## Business Understanding

### Modelo de Negocio: El "Toll Booth" mas Lucrativo del Mundo

**Que problema resuelve:**
Mastercard opera la infraestructura que permite que una transaccion con tarjeta se procese en milisegundos entre un consumidor (cardholder), un comercio (merchant), el banco del consumidor (issuer) y el banco del comercio (acquirer). Sin MA/V, el sistema de pagos global colapsaria.

**Como genera ingresos:**
```
Por cada transaccion:
- Cobran ~0.1-0.3% del valor de la transaccion (scheme fees)
- NO asumen riesgo de credito (los issuers lo asumen)
- Operan con >95% gross margin

Revenue mix 2025:
- Payment Network: ~65% (Domestic + Cross-Border Assessment)
- Value-Added Services: ~35% (Fraud, Data Analytics, etc. - +23% YoY Q4)
```

**Unit Economics:**
```
- Gross Margin: ~100% (technology company, not credit company)
- Operating Margin: 57-60% (highest in S&P 500 junto con V)
- FCF Margin: 48%
- ROIC: 44-47% (vs 9% WACC)
- Incremental ROIC: ~100% (cada dolar adicional de revenue casi todo va a FCF)
```

**Modelo de Negocio Score: 10/10**
- Asset-light (capex ~$1.2B vs $28B revenue = 4.3%)
- Near-100% FCF conversion
- Pricing power demostrado (scheme fees +2x desde 2018 pese a regulacion)
- Ingresos recurrentes (cada transaccion = toll)

## Valoracion Multi-Metodo (Tier A Methods)

### Metodo 1: Owner Earnings Yield (OEY) - Primario (60% peso)

**Calculo Owner Earnings:**
```
Free Cash Flow TTM: $16.3B
(-) Maintenance Capex Adjustment: -$0.3B (Depreciation $1.1B vs Capex $1.2B)
= Owner Earnings: ~$16.0B

Market Cap: $492B

Owner Earnings Yield = $16.0B / $492B = 3.25%
```

**Interpretacion OEY:**
```
OE Yield: 3.25%
Expected Growth: 10%
OE Yield + Growth = 13.25%

Regla Tier A: OEY + Growth > 12% = Comprar
             OEY + Growth < 10% = Esperar

>>> 13.25% > 12% = MARGINALMENTE COMPRAR

Pero esto asume NO MoS. Con MoS 15% requerido:
- Necesito OEY + Growth > 14% para comprar
- Actual: 13.25% < 14%
>>> NO COMPRAR al precio actual
```

**Fair Value via OEY:**
```
Target OEY: 4.0% (para OEY + Growth = 14%)
Owner Earnings: $16.0B
Fair Value Market Cap = $16.0B / 4.0% = $400B
```
