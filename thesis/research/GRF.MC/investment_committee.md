## META-REFLECTION

### Dudas sobre esta decision
- **The ROIC < WACC pattern is my strongest concern.** Our fund has sold 8/8 positions with this pattern. None were in structural oligopolies with visible turnaround inflection. Grifols MIGHT be the exception, but the probability is uncertain. For our fund, this uncertainty is resolved by the Tier D classification. For the friend, this is the key risk they must accept.
- **FY2025 FCF seasonality.** R2 made a strong case that Q4 FCF is historically 50%+ of annual. If this pattern holds, FY2025 FCF should meet guidance. But if it does not, the entire turnaround thesis collapses. Feb 26 is truly binary.
- **Brookfield floor may not hold.** I have used the EUR 10.50 Brookfield bid as a psychological floor. But PE bids are not price floors -- the stock can trade below. In a credit crisis or governance scandal, EUR 8 or lower is possible.
- **Am I being too generous with the R2 bull case?** Bull 7/10 is the strongest counter I have given in our system. The turnaround narrative is compelling but narratives are not data. FY2025 results are data.

### Debilidades del analisis recibido
- **CEO background error in R1:** Stated "ex-Amazon" when actually ex-Olympus Corporation of Americas. Corrected in R3. Minor but sloppy.
- **Biotest EBITDA unverified:** Neither R1 nor R2 could independently verify Biotest's current EBITDA contribution. Mason Capital's "<EUR 5M" claim may be outdated given Yimmugo launch and fibrinogen commercialization.
- **Covenant details unknown:** The specific leverage covenant thresholds are not public. "Cov-lite" is described but not quantified. This matters enormously for assessing refinancing risk.
- **Insider buying data gap:** insider_tracker.py returned minimal data for GRF.MC (Spanish listing limitation). Family 32% ownership is positive for alignment but the LACK of incremental buying at EUR 11 is a yellow flag.
- **No dedicated blood-plasma sector view.** The pharma-healthcare.md does not adequately cover the unique dynamics of the plasma oligopoly. A dedicated sector view would improve future analyses.

### Sugerencias de mejora
- **quality_scorer.py --turnaround flag:** For turnaround situations, the tool should weight forward trajectory (improving metrics) alongside backward-looking data. Currently penalizes recovering companies identically to permanently damaged ones.
- **Dedicated sector view:** Blood-plasma is a distinct subsector with unique economics (biological supply chain, donor dependency, oligopoly with extreme barriers). Should have its own sector view.
- **Leverage-adjusted ROIC:** For highly leveraged companies, provide both levered and unlevered ROIC in the QS output. The current single ROIC number is misleading when interest burden suppresses returns.
- **Precedent transaction method:** Add to valuation-methods skill as an optional cross-check. Useful for companies with recent M&A activity, rejected bids, or peer transactions.
