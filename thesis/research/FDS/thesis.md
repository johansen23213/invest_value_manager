## Business Understanding

### What Problem Does FactSet Solve?

FactSet provides financial data, analytics, and workflow technology to investment professionals. It aggregates data from hundreds of sources (market data, fundamentals, estimates, ESG, private markets) into a single integrated platform, enabling portfolio managers, analysts, traders, bankers, and wealth managers to make investment decisions, construct portfolios, and comply with regulations.

This is a **must-have** product. Investment firms cannot operate without financial data terminals/platforms. Compliance, risk management, and reporting requirements mandate access to these tools.
