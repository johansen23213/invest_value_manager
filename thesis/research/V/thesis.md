## Business Understanding

### Modelo de Negocio: El "Toll Booth" Mas Ancho del Mundo

**Que problema resuelve:**
Visa opera VisaNet, la red de pagos mas grande del mundo. Cada vez que pasas una tarjeta Visa, VisaNet autoriza, compensa y liquida la transaccion en milisegundos entre 4 partes: consumidor, comercio, banco emisor, y banco adquiriente. Sin Visa, el comercio global colapsaria.

**Como genera ingresos:**
```
Por cada transaccion Visa cobra:
- Service fees: ~0.13% del Payment Volume
- Data processing fees: ~$0.005 por transaccion
- International transaction fees: ~1% del cross-border volume

Revenue mix FY2025 (est):
- Payment Volume (67%): Crece con gasto del consumidor
- Transactions Processed (25%): Crece con digitalizacion
- Cross-Border (8%): Crece con viajes internacionales

CRITICO: Visa NO asume riesgo de credito. Los banks asumen el riesgo.
```

**Comparacion con MA:**
| Aspecto | Visa | Mastercard |
|---------|------|------------|
| Market Share | 52% | 35% |
| Cards in Circulation | 4.48B | 3.16B |
| Payment Volume | ~$15T | ~$10T |
| Revenue Model | Similar toll-booth | Similar toll-booth |
| Operating Margin | 67% | 57% |
| FCF Margin | 54% | 48% |

Visa es ~1.5x mas grande en volumen, pero MA crece ligeramente mas rapido (10% vs 8% CAGR).

**Unit Economics:**

## Valoracion Multi-Metodo (Tier A Methods)

### Metodo 1: Owner Earnings Yield (OEY) - Primario (60% peso)

**Calculo Owner Earnings:**
```
Free Cash Flow TTM: $21.6B
Depreciation (TTM): ~$1.0B
Maintenance Capex (est @ D&A * 1.1): ~$1.1B

Owner Earnings = FCF - Maintenance Capex + Depreciation
               = $21.6B - $1.1B + $1.0B
               = $21.5B

Market Cap: $632.6B

Owner Earnings Yield = $21.5B / $632.6B = 3.40%
```

**Interpretacion OEY:**
```
OE Yield: 3.40%
Expected Growth: 9% (FCF growth, includes buybacks)
OE Yield + Growth = 12.40%

Regla Tier A: OEY + Growth > 12% = Comprar
             OEY + Growth < 10% = Esperar

>>> 12.40% > 12% = MARGINALMENTE COMPRAR

Pero con MoS 12% requerido:
- Necesito OEY + Growth > 13.5% para comprar
- Actual: 12.40% < 13.5%
>>> NO COMPRAR al precio actual
```

**Fair Value via OEY:**
```
Target OEY: 4.0% (para OEY + Growth = 13%)
Owner Earnings: $21.5B
Fair Value Market Cap = $21.5B / 4.0% = $537.5B

Current Market Cap: $632.6B
```
