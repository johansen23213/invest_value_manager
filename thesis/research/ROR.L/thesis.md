## Business Understanding

### What Does Rotork Do?

Rotork designs, manufactures, and services **flow control equipment** -- primarily valve actuators and related instrumentation. Actuators are the devices that open, close, and regulate valves in industrial processes. These are mission-critical components: if an actuator fails on a pipeline, refinery, water treatment plant, or power station, the consequences range from production shutdown to environmental disaster.

**Revenue breakdown (FY2024: GBP 754.4M):**

| Segment | Revenue Est. | Description |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Oil & Gas | ~35-40% | Upstream, midstream, downstream |
| Water & Power | ~25-30% | Municipal water, power generation |
| Chemical/Process Industries (CPI) | ~20-25% | Chemicals, mining, food/beverage |
| Service/Aftermarket | ~23% | Growing -- maintenance, spares, digital services |

**Revenue model:**
- Equipment sales (new actuators): ~60-65% of revenue
- Aftermarket/Service: ~23% and growing (sticky, recurring, higher margin)
- Site Services (Rotork Service): Installation, commissioning, lifecycle management
- Software/Digital: Intelligent asset management (IQ3 platform, Pakscan networking)

**Geographic diversification:**
- EMEA: ~35%
- Americas: ~30%
- Asia Pacific: ~35%

### Unit Economics

- **Gross Margin: 49.3%** (expanding from 46.2% in 2021) -- premium engineering with pricing power
- **Operating Margin: 22.0%** (H1 2025 adjusted, expanding 140bps YoY)
- **FCF Margin: 17.1%** (up from 11.5% in 2021 -- significant improvement)
- **FCF Conversion: 1.4x net income** (2024) -- excellent earnings quality
- **ROIC: 22.1%** vs WACC 9.4% = **+12.7pp spread** -- value creation machine
- **Capital intensity: LOW** -- Capex/Revenue ~3.5%, asset-light manufacturing model
- **Net Cash: GBP 43M** -- pristine balance sheet

### Moat Sources

1. **Installed Base Lock-In (STRONGEST):** Rotork has 5M+ actuators installed globally. Each actuator has a 20-30 year operational life. Once installed, the switching cost is enormous -- new actuator, new wiring, recertification, process downtime. The aftermarket/service revenue (23% and growing) is the monetization of this installed base.

2. **Safety/Regulatory Certification:** Products must meet stringent safety certifications (SIL rated, ATEX certified for explosive environments). Competitor products cannot simply be substituted without re-certification -- this takes years.

3. **Brand/Specification:** Engineers specify "Rotork" by name in project specs, similar to how architects specify specific brands. Once specified, the procurement team buys Rotork regardless of competitor alternatives.

4. **Technology Leadership:** IQ3 intelligent actuator platform with built-in diagnostics, Bluetooth connectivity, predictive maintenance capability. Moving from "dumb" actuator to digital asset management platform -- this increases switching costs further.

5. **Global Service Network:** Rotork Service operates in 50+ countries. For mission-critical infrastructure, local service availability is non-negotiable.

**Moat Assessment: NARROW-to-WIDE.** The installed base + certification moat is durable. ROIC persistence at >18% for 4+ consecutive years confirms the moat generates economic value. The moat is not as wide as a monopoly platform (like AUTO.L) but wider than typical industrial companies.
