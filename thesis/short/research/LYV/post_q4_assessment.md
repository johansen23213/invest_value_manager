## 6. Additional Observations

### TM Concentration Declining — Structural Shift
The most important datapoint is **Ticketmaster AOI declining from 52% to 45.8%** of total. This contradicts a core thesis pillar that TM is the sole profit engine. Concerts AOI grew +30% — the live events side is becoming more profitable. If this trend continues, the "fragility if TM is forced to divest" argument weakens because Concert economics can partially compensate.

Stock gained 2.46% after-hours to $161.25, but appears to have given back most gains in regular session (now $157.46). Two interpretations:
- **Bull:** Market already priced in the beat; antitrust risk caps upside
