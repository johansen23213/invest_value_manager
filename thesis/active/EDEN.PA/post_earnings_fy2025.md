**UNCHANGED.** Reported EBITDA -8% to -12% (rebasing year). Intrinsic growth +8/+12%. The delta is entirely attributable to Italy commission cap + Brazil decree -- the underlying business continues compounding.

**CONFIRMED.** 2027-2028 target +8/+12% EBITDA growth MAINTAINED. 2030 ambition EUR 5B revenue (from EUR 2,961M = ~69% growth over 4 years, implying ~14% CAGR).

**BEAT.** DPS EUR 1.33 (+10% YoY vs EUR 1.21 FY2024). Above consensus EUR 1.306 (+1.8%). At EUR 18.98 = yield 7.0%.

### 7. Italy 5% cap impact quantified? (esperamos ~EUR 120M annual)
Italy 5% cap impact approximately EUR 120M as expected. No upside surprise on legal challenge, but no worse-than-expected scenario either. Impact contained within original adversarial estimates.

### 8. Italy fraud investigation update?
**NO ESCALATION.** No material new developments on Rome prosecution. No additional seizures, no trial verdict. Status: ONGOING but not worsening.

### 9. Brazil decree impact update?
Brazil absorbed within guidance. LatAm overall: +13.2% LFL revenue (Brazil +15.7%). Despite decree uncertainty, Brazil delivered strong topline growth. Injunction for Ticket SA still active.

FCF/EBITDA conversion 82% (target was 70%) = MASSIVE outperformance. Net Debt reduced from EUR 1,806M to EUR 1,241M (-31%). Net Debt/EBITDA dropped from ~1.4x to 0.9x. Balance sheet dramatically stronger than expected.
