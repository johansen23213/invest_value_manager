### KILL CONDITION #6: ITALY DOUBLE THREAT (NUEVO v2)

**A) 5% Commission Cap on Meal Vouchers:**
- Law approved by Italian parliament. Phased: existing accords from Sep 1, 2025. ALL vouchers from Jan 1, 2026.
- Commissions capped at 5% of face value (current: up to 20%)
- Impact: ~EUR 60M EBITDA in 2025, ~EUR 120M annually ongoing
- Edenred stated will challenge at Italian administrative court
- **STATUS: ACTIVE. In force since Jan 1, 2026.**

**B) Fraud/Bid-Rigging Investigation:**
- Rome Prosecutor investigating Edenred Italia management for fraud + bid-rigging
- EUR 20M seized. 4 individuals + company accused
- Public sector meal voucher tender worth EUR 580M in 4 lots
- Period: 2021-2023. Prosecutors requesting trial for former executives.
- **STATUS: ONGOING. Monitor for settlement/conviction/fine.**

**KC#6 TRIGGER:** If Italy combined impact exceeds EUR 150M EBITDA AND legal penalties/fines materialize → SELL.
