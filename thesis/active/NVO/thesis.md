### KC#1 DETAILED ASSESSMENT

**What REDEFINE 4 showed:**
- CagriSema 2.4/2.4mg: 23.0% weight loss at 84 weeks (efficacy estimand)
- Tirzepatide 15mg: 25.5% weight loss at 84 weeks
- Treatment-regimen estimand (real-world adherence): 20.2% vs 23.6%
- Primary endpoint of non-inferiority: NOT MET

**What this means for the thesis:**
1. CagriSema is NOT best-in-class. Tirzepatide (Eli Lilly) is clearly superior on weight loss.
2. CagriSema is still SIGNIFICANTLY better than Wegovy alone (23% vs 16% weight loss).
3. CagriSema standalone FDA filing is INTACT -- based on REDEFINE 1 (23% vs placebo) and REDEFINE 2, not REDEFINE 4.
4. FDA decision expected late 2026. Approval likely (efficacy is strong vs placebo).
5. REDEFINE 11 (higher-dose CagriSema) expected H1 2027 -- could partially recover the narrative.
6. Goldman slashed CagriSema sales estimate from $11.8B to $5B. Revenue ceiling is LOWER.

**Why HOLD, not SELL:**
- The original thesis had a DUAL exit condition: KC#1 triggered PLUS FY2026 guidance miss >10%.
- Only KC#1 is triggered. FY2026 guidance has not yet been updated post-REDEFINE 4.
- At P/E 10.1x, the market is pricing in WORSE than our bear case.
- Selling at 52-week low ($35.85 recent) locks in loss without catalyst.
- Oral Wegovy + Medicare coverage are POSITIVE offsets not priced in.
