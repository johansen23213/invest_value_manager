## 3. ESCENARIOS DE EARNINGS

### BULL (probabilidad 20%)
**Revenue >2%, FCF beat, optimistic AI commentary**

Que buscar:
- Revenue growth >=2% (beats revised guidance)
- FCF >EUR 1.1B (beats expectations)
- EBITDA margin 15%+
- Positive commentary on AI transformation ("Centaur Strategy" progressing)
- Contract wins announced
- 2026 guidance +2-4%

**Implicacion:** Tesis muy fortalecida. Market over-pessimistic confirmado. HOLD con conviccion.

### BASE (probabilidad 50%)
**Revenue 1-2%, FCF ~EUR 1B, guidance cautious**

Que buscar:
- Revenue +1-2% (within guidance)
- FCF EUR 900M-EUR 1.1B
- EBITDA margin 14.7-15%
- Mixed AI commentary ("adapting", "challenges but managing")
- 2026 guidance flat to +2%
- Dividend maintained

**Implicacion:** Tesis intacta. No es terminal decline. HOLD.

### BEAR (probabilidad 30%)
**Revenue flat/negative, FCF miss, guidance cut**

Que buscar:
- Revenue flat or <1%
- FCF <EUR 900M
- EBITDA margin <14.5%
- Negative AI commentary (major client losses to AI)
- 2026 guidance cut to flat/negative
- Dividend at risk comments

**Implicacion:** Re-evaluar inmediatamente. Si es one-time, HOLD. Si es structural decline, considerar EXIT.

3. **Revenue per employee trend?**

   - Si cayendo -> AI siendo threat no tool
