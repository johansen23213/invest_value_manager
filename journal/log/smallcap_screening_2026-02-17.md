- **Not in universe**: CORRECT -- new discovery from FTSE AIM100

### 2. WDFC - WD-40 Company (TIER A, QS 75) -- STRONG CANDIDATE
- **Business**: Iconic household brand, 87% of revenue international, asset-light model
- **Quality**: ROIC 30%, 55% GM expanding, 6% rev CAGR, near net cash
- **Moat**: Brand recognition, dominant market share, 65%+ international exposure
- **Valuation**: P/E 37.2x (premium, but consistent compounder). 3% off 52w high
- **Risk**: Single product concentration, slow growth, P/E premium
- **Not in universe**: CORRECT -- discovered via SP600 screening

- **Quality**: ROIC 117%, 91% GM, 77% operating margin, net cash, 54% FCF margin
