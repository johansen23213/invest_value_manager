# Sector: Alternative Asset Management

> Ultima actualizacion: 2026-02-19
> Status: NEUTRAL (selectively attractive within infrastructure/real assets niche)

## Resumen Ejecutivo

Alternative asset management is a structurally growing segment within financial services. Total UK-managed assets reached GBP 10 trillion in 2024, with alternatives growing fastest at ~15% CAGR through 2031. Key drivers include: (1) institutional investors rotating from traditional assets into infrastructure, private equity, and real assets; (2) DC pension scheme mandates (Mansion House Accord) directing capital to private markets; (3) energy transition infrastructure requiring massive capital deployment. Fee compression in traditional asset management (ETF/passive shift) does NOT apply equally to alternatives, where alpha generation and illiquidity premium justify higher fee structures. However, fundraising cyclicality, performance fee variability, and key-person risk remain sector risks. Listed alternative managers trade at varying multiples depending on AUM stickiness, fee durability, and performance track record.
