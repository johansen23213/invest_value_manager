## Thesis Summary

**Core thesis:**
- Energy supermajor integrada, LNG dominante global (#1 mundial)
- Cotiza ~7x P/FCF, genera $40B+ FCF anual
- Buybacks $3.5B/trimestre (16 trimestres consecutivos >$3B), dividend yield 3.5-4%
- Balance sólido (deuda neta $41B, rating AA-, cobertura intereses 11.9x)
- **Hedge geopolítico:** Irán tensions → oil +30-50% = Shell upside masivo
- Management pragmático (Sawan): gas/LNG >renovables, FCF >growth verde
- Margen seguridad >30%

**Key risks:**
- Transición energética largo plazo (10-15 años runway OK)
- Precio commodity volatility (oil $70-80 actual, FCF sostenible >$60)
- Regulación ESG/windfall taxes EU
- Peak oil demand 2035-40 (gradual, no cliff)

**Valuation:**
- Fair value: £40-50 (~€46-58) → margen seguridad 30-50%
- P/FCF 7x vs sector 8-10x = barato
