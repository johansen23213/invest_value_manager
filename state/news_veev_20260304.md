## Assessment

### Classification: MATERIAL (not yet CRITICAL)

The stock has dropped ~$40 from ~$222 to ~$183 over the past 2 months, driven by legitimate fundamental concerns (CRM erosion, insider selling, Goldman downgrade). This is NOT noise -- the CRM share loss from 18 to 14 of top-20 is a real structural concern that our DA already flagged.

However, the stock is still 21-26% ABOVE our entry price of $145. Even in a moderately bearish earnings scenario, it is unlikely to reach our entry range in one move. The business remains genuinely high-quality (75% gross margins, 40% FCF margins, $6.5B net cash), and the $2B buyback provides a floor.

### Key Question for Tonight
The critical data points to watch in the Q4 release:
1. **CRM retention update**: Any change from "14 or so" of top-20?
2. **Subscription revenue growth**: Decelerating from 16%? To what level?
3. **FY2027 guidance**: Revenue and subscription growth outlook
4. **Billings growth**: Leading indicator for future revenue
5. **Management commentary on Salesforce competition**
